1.) Open docker desktop and start docker.
2.) Connect the motor driver to laptop, then open powershell as administrator and follow step 8 to bind and attach busid.
3.) Follow step 9 to verify the port. (should be /dev/ttyACM0, if anything else, then must have to change in docker-compose.yml (refer step 10)).
4.) Then in powershell, type:wsl ,then inside wsl, type: cd POC_arm, then follow step 11 to open container.
5.) Follow step 12 to build and source the workspace.
6.) You can follow step 13 and 15 to verify all servo ids are present and currently at 0.
7.) Then type: cd..  to go back to ros2_ws and lastly follow step 16. This will launch moveit launch file and then you can try out some poses.
